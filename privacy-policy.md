# Privacy Policy

**Volume Vision**
Effective Date: May 4, 2026

## Overview

Volume Vision is designed to keep your data on your device. We do not collect, transmit, or store any personal information.

## Data That Stays On Your Device

All of the following remain entirely on your hardware:

- RGB and LiDAR depth captures
- Annotations (bounding boxes and polygon masks)
- Trained machine learning models and training history
- Exported CoreML model files
- App settings and preferences

None of this data is transmitted to any server.

## Device Permissions

Volume Vision requests the following permissions, each of which you can decline:

- **Camera and LiDAR** — Required to capture RGB images and depth data for annotation and training.
- **Photo Library** — Used to save and import capture images.
- **Microphone** — Requested for planned audio classification features. Not active in the current release.

## What We Do Not Do

- We do not collect analytics or usage data.
- We do not use tracking or advertising frameworks.
- We do not display ads.
- We do not require account creation.
- We do not share data with third parties.
- We do not use cookies.

## Data Storage

All data is stored locally in the app's sandboxed container on your device. Removing the app removes all associated data.

## Children's Privacy

Volume Vision does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a revised effective date.
