# Volume Vision

On-device machine learning for LiDAR-based measurement.

Capture objects with RGB and LiDAR depth. Annotate with bounding boxes or polygon masks. Train detection and segmentation models directly on your iPhone or iPad. Export to CoreML.

Everything stays on your device. No cloud. No account. No tracking.
