# Volume Vision

On-device machine learning for LiDAR-based object detection and measurement.

Capture objects with RGB and LiDAR depth. Annotate with bounding boxes or polygon masks. Train detection models directly on your iPhone or iPad using a compiled Metal training pipeline. Export to CoreML.

**Dual-input architecture** — Train models that fuse RGB and depth data for detection that goes beyond what cameras alone can see. Or train RGB-only models when depth data isn't available.

Everything stays on your device. No cloud. No account. No tracking.

Volume Vision is available on the App Store for $9.99.
